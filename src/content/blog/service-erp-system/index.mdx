开源ERP系统部署和开发, 包括erpnext, odoo. 


erpnext系统的核心模块有:
1. 采购管理
2. 库存与仓储（Stock）
3. 制造管理（Manufacturing）
4. 财务管理（Accounts）
5. 销售与 CRM（Selling & CRM）
6. 人力资源（HR）
7. 项目管理（Projects）
8. 质量管控（Quality）
9. 供应商管理（Supplier）
10. 其他常用模块. 资产维护（Maintenance）, 零售, 网站 / 电商（Website）, 报表 / 仪表盘（Dashboard）.

Our core service focuses on customized development and full deployment of two mainstream open-source ERP platforms: ERPNext and Odoo.
Our full-spectrum technical solutions contain:
ERP system deployment: On-premise, private cloud and public cloud environment setup, server configuration, database deployment and environment optimization;
Secondary development & customization: Business workflow adjustment, self-developed functional modules, page & report customization, UI/UX optimization, logic modification matching corporate personalized management demands;
Cross-system integration: API interface development, data docking with e-commerce platforms, payment gateways, logistics systems, OA and other third-party software;
Post-launch services: System troubleshooting, version upgrade, data migration, regular operation & maintenance and long-term technical consultation.
We tailor ERP management systems for trading, manufacturing, service and retail enterprises to digitalize internal operation, finance, supply chain, production and customer management.
